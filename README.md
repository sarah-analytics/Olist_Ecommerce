# Olist E-commerce Analytics  
 MySQL 路 Tableau 路 Marketplace KPI Analysis

A concise analytics project examining key marketplace metrics across orders, revenue, delivery performance, product categories, and customer behavior.

---

##  Dataset  
Brazilian Olist public dataset.

---

##  KPIs  
Standard e-commerce metrics analyzed:

1. Daily Orders  
2. Daily Revenue  
3. Category Revenue Top 10  
4. Delivery On-time Rate  
5. Weekly Revenue Trend  
6. 30-day Repeat Retention  
7. Average Delivery Days  
8. Payment Type Mix  
9. Price Distribution  
10. Best-selling Products  

---

##  Tableau Dashboard  
Dashboard is organized into four analytical views:

### **1) Overview**  
Daily orders 路 daily revenue 路 short-term trend indicators

### **2) Operations**  
On-time delivery rate 路 average delivery days

### **3) Merchandising**  
Category revenue 路 product performance 路 pricing patterns

### **4) Customer**  
New vs repeat buyers 路 30-day repeat retention

---

##  Insights  

**Overview**  
Order volume and revenue show clear seasonal peaks.

**Operations**  
Longer delivery times correlate with higher late-delivery counts.

**Merchandising**  
A small number of categories and SKUs drive most of the revenue; pricing forms distinct clusters.

**Customer**  
Retention is present but moderate, showing opportunities for re-engagement.
